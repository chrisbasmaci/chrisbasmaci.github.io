After starting with university, I started using the new knowledge I've gained to propel our store. My next initiative was to modernize our shop menu while keeping the authentic and oriental aspects of our shop. With the new design elements and a modernized layout I put together a new menu from scratch.

My final initiative for our store is to create a website for ecommerce. The skeleton of our site is almost finished, however it's still a work in progress.
